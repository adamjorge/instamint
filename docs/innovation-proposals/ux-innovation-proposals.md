# UX Innovation Proposals

## 1st proposal : Integration of Account Abstraction

### Description:

Account Abstraction simplifies the user experience by combining the functionalities of user accounts and smart contracts into a single abstract account. This eliminates the traditional distinction between externally owned accounts (used by users) and smart contract accounts. Here are some ways this integration can be realized:

- **Smart Wallets**: Create wallets that automatically manage complex transactions in the background, allowing users to execute actions with a single click.
- **Multisignature and granular authorizations**: Implement systems for multisignature and granular authorizations, enabling secure transactions authorized by multiple parties or devices.
- **Third-party fee payment**: Allow third parties to pay transaction fees, simplifying the user experience by eliminating the need to hold specific cryptocurrencies for fees.
- **Account recovery**: Offer enhanced account recovery mechanisms, allowing users to regain access to their accounts in case of private key loss, through social approvals or pre-established backup methods.

### Benefits:

- **Ease of use**: Users can interact with decentralized applications (dApps) without worrying about underlying technical complexities, making blockchain accessible to a broader audience.
- **Enhanced security**: Multisignature and granular authorizations add additional layers of security, protecting users from unauthorized transactions.
- **Flexibility**: The ability for third parties to pay transaction fees and improved account recovery mechanisms offer greater flexibility, effectively addressing user needs.
- **Optimized UX**: By combining user accounts and smart contracts, users benefit from a more seamless and integrated experience, increasing satisfaction and retention.

## 2nd proposal: Monetization of creator content

### Description:

Enable creators to monetize their content by offering paid content options. This feature allows creators to generate revenue from their creations and build a dedicated community. Here are some key aspects of this integration:

- **Paid content access**: Allow creators to set up content that requires payment to access, providing them with a direct revenue stream from their audience.
- **Subscription models**: Introduce subscription options where users can subscribe to creators for exclusive content, fostering a steady income for creators.
- **Tiered memberships**: Implement tiered membership levels offering varying access to content and perks, encouraging users to invest more for additional benefits.
- **In-App purchases**: Allow creators to offer in-app purchases for unique content, features, or experiences, enhancing the user engagement and monetization potential.

### Benefits:

- **Revenue generation**: Creators can earn money directly from their content, providing a sustainable financial model for their work.
- **Community building**: By offering exclusive content, creators can attract and maintain a loyal community of supporters who are willing to pay for premium access.
- **Enhanced user engagement**: Paid content and subscription models increase user engagement as they invest in and interact with exclusive offerings from their favorite creators.
- **Flexible monetization options**: Offering multiple monetization methods, such as one-time payments, subscriptions, and in-app purchases, allows creators to choose the best strategy for their audience and content type.
- **Platform profitability**: By taking a small fee from transactions, the platform can generate its own revenue, ensuring the continuous improvement and maintenance of the service.
